How to make AI-generated images

  1. Start a design project from scratch or with a template. On the editor, click “Elements,“ then scroll down to “AI image generator.” Alternatively, head to the homepage search bar, click “Canva AI,” and then choose “Create an image“ before typing your prompt.
  2. Describe the image you’d like to generate. The more detail you can provide, the better.
  3. If using Magic Media’s Text to Image, you can also choose an image style from our available options like Watercolor, Filmic, Neon, Color Pencil, and Retrowave. Then, click ‘Create your image.’
  4. Finish up the AI-generated image by adding effects, filters, and other elements from our library.
  5. Download your AI-generated images, use them on your other Canva projects, or share them directly from Canva on your online platforms.
